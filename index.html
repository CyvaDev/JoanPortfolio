About Me

Hi! I'm Joan, a young and passionate game designer specializing in Level Design and UI Design. I've been fascinated by video games since I was a kid, and for years I dreamed of becoming an architect until I discovered the world of Game Design.

That’s when I found my true calling: creating interactive experiences that blend visual design, storytelling, and gameplay.

Right now, I’m focused on sharpening my skills as a Level Designer, crafting playable spaces that not only challenge players but also tell stories and enhance immersion.

I'm also deeply interested in UI Design, as I believe a clear and intuitive interface is essential for a smooth and enjoyable gaming experience.

I'm always looking for new challenges, collaborations, and projects where I can grow as a professional and bring my creativity to the table.
